# Posters Folder

Place your poster presentation files here.

## File Naming Convention
- `king-lab-poster.pdf` or `.png` - For Michael R. King Laboratory poster
- `franz-lab-poster.pdf` or `.png` - For Franz Research Group poster

## Supported Formats
- PDF (.pdf) - Recommended for best quality
- PNG (.png) - High resolution image
- JPG (.jpg) - High resolution image

## Usage
After adding your poster files, update the `poster` field in the Projects component for the corresponding research project.
